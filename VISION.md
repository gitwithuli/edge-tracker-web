## Core Differentiators

### 1. Edge-First Architecture
TradeZella tracks trades. We track **strategies**.

Every trade belongs to an edge. Every edge has its own stats. Compare edges. Kill the losers. Scale the winners.

### 2. AI Screenshot Parser
Paste a TradingView screenshot → AI extracts entry, SL, TP, R:R, outcome.

No manual entry. No friction. Just trade and log.

### 3. ICT Native
Built for ICT/SMC traders:
- Session tracking (Asian, London, NY AM/PM)
- PD Array tagging (FVG, OB, Breaker)
- SMT divergence logging
- Liquidity event tracking
- Kill zone detection

### 4. Backtest vs Live Comparison
See if your live performance matches your backtest. Side by side. Same edge.

### 5. Better Price
$29/mo vs $49/mo. Same features. Better UX.
